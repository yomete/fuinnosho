## 100 Daily Prompts

### Days 1-10: Foundation Building (Sep 11-20)

**Day 1 - First Frame Ritual**  
*Current Roll: Load Portra 160*  
Take your first exposure of this challenge. Photograph something that represents your current photographic mindset. What do you want to achieve in these 100 days?

**Day 2 - Golden Hour Hunt**  
*Portra 160 - Frame 2-3*  
Capture the golden hour in color. Study how Portra 160 renders warm light and skin tones.

**Day 3 - Rule of Thirds Rebellion**  
*Portra 160 - Frame 4-5*  
Deliberately break the rule of thirds. Center your subject, use extreme compositions. What stories emerge from unconventional framing?

**Day 4 - Color Temperature Study**  
*Portra 160 - Frame 6-8*  
Photograph the same subject in different light sources. Window light vs. tungsten vs. daylight. How does Portra handle mixed lighting?

**Day 5 - Portrait Practice**  
*Portra 160 - Frame 9-11*  
Portra's designed for portraits. Practice with available light, focus on skin tone rendering.

**Day 6 - Street Level**  
*Portra 160 - Frame 12-15*  
Get low. Photograph from knee-height or ground level. How does this perspective change familiar scenes?

**Day 7 - Overcast Color**  
*Portra 160 - Frame 16-18*  
Portra shines in overcast conditions. Explore how it handles muted, soft light.

**Day 8 - Bulk Loading Preparation Day**  
*Portra 160 - Frame 19-22*  
While shooting these frames, research bulk loading tutorials. Order film changing bag if needed. Plan your HP5 bulk loading setup.

**Day 9 - Motion and Color**  
*Portra 160 - Frame 23-26*  
Experiment with motion blur in color. How does color affect the perception of movement?

**Day 10 - Detail Hunt**  
*Portra 160 - Frame 27-30*  
Focus on small details others miss. How does Portra's fine grain help with detailed work?
